Felicien Ihirwe, PhD

I’m a Research Engineer at FlandersMake vzw, part of the CodesignS core lab in Leuven, Belgium. My work focuses on applying Linked Data Intelligence, Datapace, Digital Product Passport, and Low-Code Engineering in the Digital Manufacturing domain. Previously, I worked as a model-based developer at Intecs Solutions and have experience in embedded software research, functional safety, and industrial IoT. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of L’Aquila and a Master’s in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University.

To learn more about me, you can send me an email . Don't forget to check on my Twitter , LinkedIn , Google Scholar , ResearchGate , and GitHub , CV, list of publications, and my other research activities.
